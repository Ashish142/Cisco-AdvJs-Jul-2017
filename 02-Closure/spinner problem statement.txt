create an object and assign it to a variable 'spinner'

the object must exhibit the following behavior

spinner.up() //=> 1
spinner.up() //=> 2
spinner.up() //=> 3
spinner.up() //=> 4

spinner.down() //=> 3
spinner.down() //=> 2
spinner.down() //=> 1
spinner.down() //=> 0
spinner.down() //=> -1

Note : the outcome should NOT be influenced through any other means than by invoking the up an down methods.
